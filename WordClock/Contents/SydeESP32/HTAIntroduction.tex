\chapter{HTA – Hybrid Traction Assistance}
Hybrid Traction Assistance (HTA) \ref{fig:HTAPatent} is an intelligent system designed to reduce the physical effort required to move heavy loads. It automatically adjusts traction in both forward and reverse movement by monitoring the speed and terrain conditions. HTA actively balances forces during uphill and downhill motion—providing additional assistance when moving uphill and applying braking when moving downhill to prevent uncontrolled acceleration \ref{fig:HTA}.

What has so far been an electrical assistance system for animal-drawn vehicles or machines is now intended to be adapted for use in a pallet truck. The initial concept is to transfer the previously proven mechanism to the new application: load cells measure the pressure or pulling force applied to the pallet truck and transmit this information to motors. The motors then either assist or counteract the force, depending on the movement conditions, to support the operator.
